How to pay via ATM BCA/PRIMA network:
  1. Enter your PIN.
  2. On the main menu, choose ''Other Transaction'.
  3. Choose 'Transfer'.
  4. Choose 'Other Bank Account'.
  5. Enter '013' for Bank Permata then press 'Correct'.
  6. Enter the amount to be paid, then choose 'Correct'. Note, if the amount entered is not the same as the invoiced amount, the transaction will be denied.
  7. Enter (the 16 digit account number) then press 'Correct'.
  8. Amount to be paid & account number will appear on the payment confirmation page. If the information is correct, press 'Correct'.
  9. Transaction Success.
How to pay via ATM Mandiri/ATM Bersama network:
  1. Enter your PIN.
  2. On the main menu, choose 'Others'.
  3. Choose 'Transfer'.
  4. Choose 'Online Transfer'.
  5. Enter the number 013 (code 013 and 16 digit account number).
  6. The reference number can be left blank, and then choose 'Correct'.
  7. Enter the amount to be paid, then choose 'Correct'. Note, if the amount entered is not the same as the invoiced amount, the transaction will be denied.
  8. Amount to be paid & account number will appear on the payment confirmation page. If the information is correct, press 'Correct'.
  9. Transaction Success.
How to pay via ATM BCA/PRIMA network:
  1. Enter your PIN.
  2. On the main menu, choose 'Other Transaction'.
  3. Choose 'Payment'.
  4. Choose 'Other Payment'.
  5. Choose 'Virtual Account'.
  6. Please input the 16 digit Account Number
  7. Amount to be paid & account number will appear on the payment confirmation page. If the information is correct, press 'Correct'.
  8. Choose your payment account and then press 'Correct'.
